FAQ

We're still building Pay Split, but here are a few answers to common questions

Who is this for?

Anyone who needs to split incoming payments: it will be most useful for people creating products and events together. It can also be used as an affiliate system for paying out commisions, or a daily deals site (like AppSumo or Dealotto) that needs to pay out earnings.

What gateways are supported?

Currently, we only plan on supporting Stripe. Each account will need a master Stripe account, and then separate accounts for each partner.
